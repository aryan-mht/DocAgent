Academic policies
Please note that no new applications will be accepted at this time.
The Indian Teacher Education Program (ITEP) also offers the Aboriginal Teacher Associate Certificate which is designed to provide sufficient post-secondary study in education to enable certificate holders in assisting classroom teachers in Aboriginal language instruction, cross-cultural education, and in meeting the unique and culturally specific needs of Aboriginal students.
