Academic policies
Students enrolled in various B.S.A. degree programs find themselves in occupations where in-depth knowledge of microbiology is expected or highly complementary (e.g., to the agricultural (soils, crops, animals and plants), environmental and engineering sciences). The Applied Microbiology Minor addresses this need.
