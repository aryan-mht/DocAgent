Academic policies
The Bachelor of Science in Dental Therapy [B.Sc.(DT)] program consists of 24 months of continuous, intensive learning. There are two pathways that can be completed in order to achieve the B.Sc.(DT) program. One involves direct entry from high school and the other involves admission of Provincially-registered (licensed) dental hygienists with a Diploma, Certificate, or B.Sc. in Dental Hygiene. Admission pathways for each are outlined in the Admission Requirements section of this Catalogue. The following outlines the program of study for each pathway:
