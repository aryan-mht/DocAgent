Academic policies
The Department of Educational Psychology and Special Education, University of Saskatchewan, currently offers two M.Ed. (Thesis-based) programs:  (1) M.Ed. in School & Counselling Psychology; and (2) M.Ed. with a specialization in either Special Education or Measurement and Evaluation, as well as the M.Ed. Course-based option in Educational Psychology and Special Education in either Special Education or Measurement and Evaluation.
The
Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Education
is offered by the College of Education through a cross-departmental Ph.D. program.
For more information on this field of study, see the
Department of Educational Psychology and Special Education
website.
