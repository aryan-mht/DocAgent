Academic policies
Diploma in Educational Studies (90 credit units)
Students are not required to apply for this diploma program. Undergraduate students enrolled in Year 3 of a Bachelor of Education program route will be given the opportunity to transfer into the Diploma in Educational Studies.
Requirements (90 credit units)
84 credit units from the Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) program route in which the student was previously enrolled.
Choose 6 credit units of Education Electives (can be EDST courses or in place of EDST requirements):
EADM — 100-Level, 200-Level, 300-Level, 400-Level
ECUR — 100-Level, 200-Level, 300-Level, 400-Level
EDST — 100-Level, 200-Level, 300-Level, 400-Level
