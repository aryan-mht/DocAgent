Academic policies
Educational Technology and Design, housed in the Department of Curriculum Studies, is an academic field of study and practice concerned with the social, professional and pedagogical influence of technology in learning. It is multidisciplinary in nature, drawing theoretical perspectives and principles from psychology, communications theory, systems theory, learning science, computer and information technology, and informatics.
