Academic policies
The Engineering Innovation and Entrepreneurship Certificate program introduces students to business fundamentals and to the processes necessary to design and commercialize technologically-innovative solutions. Students who complete the program will have the knowledge, skills, and abilities necessary to identify good problems involving tech innovation opportunities, to design solutions to those problems, and to commercialize their innovations.
Students in the Engineering Innovation and Entrepreneurship program will be able to gain additional experience through the award winning USask SIGMA Educational Skill Accelerator (
https://engineering.usask.ca/departments/sigma.php
), and other co-curricular activities offered by the Tech Innovation Program. For more information, please visit the
program website
.
