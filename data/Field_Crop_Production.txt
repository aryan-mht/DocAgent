Academic policies
The Field Crop Production minor is an approved minor field of study in the B.S.A. degree and the B.Sc.(Agbus.) Honours and B.Sc.(Agbus.) degrees. It
cannot be combined with a major in a related area.
A minor consists of 18 credit units in a field of study outside the student's major. At least 12 credit units in the minor must be courses that are not specifically listed as required in a student's major.
