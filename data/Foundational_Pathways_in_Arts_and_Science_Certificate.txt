Academic policies
The Foundational Pathways in Arts and Science certificate is designed to capture the course areas that are needed for all Arts and Science programs, ultimately setting students up for success if they continue into a degree program, while also allowing students to explore their area(s) of interest and specialization.
This certificate may only be received before the student receives any degree. It may not be received at the same Convocation as the student will receive a degree, even if the degree will be awarded at a later ceremony.
