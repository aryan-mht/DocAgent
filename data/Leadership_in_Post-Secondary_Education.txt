The Certificate in Leadership in Post-Secondary Education is a 12 credit unit degree-level certificate offered at the undergraduate level through the Department of Educational Administration, College of Education. The program investigates the post-secondary environment and focuses on three main topic areas: administration and governance, teaching and learning, and student services. Students in this program will be required to participate in a capstone activity that incorporates their new understandings, and applications to post-secondary institutions, into a culminating presentation.
