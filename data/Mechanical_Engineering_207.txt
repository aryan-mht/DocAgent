Academic policies
Research is conducted in various disciplines of Mechanical Engineering which include: Applied Mechanics, Materials and Metallurgy, Manufacturing, Fluid and Thermal Sciences, Bio-engineering, Robotics,and Control Systems. Multidisciplinary studies are encouraged with the cooperation of other departments. Experimental, numerical, and theoretical investigations are carried out using a large number of research labs and computer systems.
