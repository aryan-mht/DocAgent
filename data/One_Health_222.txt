Admission Requirements
Language Proficiency Requirements: Proof of English proficiency may be required for international applicants and for applicants whose first language is not English.
Students must be registered in a Master's or Ph.D. program. A cumulative weighted average of at least 70% (U of S grade system equivalent) in the last two years of study is required.

Certificate Requirements
This program consists of the following courses, totaling 6 credit units, to be completed during full-time enrolment during a one-year period, including:
GPS 960.0
GPS 961.0, if research involves human subjects
GPS 962.0, if research involves animal subjects
ONEH 800.3
ONEH 801.3
Please note:
No double-counting is permitted. The 6 credit units cannot be credited in a program other than the Graduate Certificate in One Health.
