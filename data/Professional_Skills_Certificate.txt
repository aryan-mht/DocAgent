Academic policies
The Graduate Professional Skills Certificate (GPSC) is a comprehensive, non-credit program that develops graduate students’ professional skills with a focus on critical thinking, communication and teamwork, management and leadership, entrepreneurship and career development, and ethics. Rich in interactive, experiential, and case-based learning opportunities, this is one of the few such certificates in Canada.
Admission Requirements
Registration in a graduate program or as a U of S postdoctoral fellow.
Certificate Requirements
Experiential learning opportunities (workshops, internships, courses, and other experiences completed to the extent that achievement of learning outcomes in GSR 974 can be demonstrated (mapped to 10 skill domains) with artifacts and a reflective commentary)
